Prize Winners in Transportation Survey

Nearly two dozen employees won prizes by taking Parking and Transportation Services’ 2014 UC Davis Health System Transportation Survey.

Parking and Transportation Services conducted the survey in November to obtain information on how employees on the Sacramento campus commute to work. Approximately 4,500 individuals participated in the survey, providing valuable information for planning purposes. Many respondents indicated their interest in a commute that reduces stress as well as cost.

Transportation

Med-Transit (On-Site) Shuttle Services

UC Davis Health System's Med-Transit Shuttle Service offers convenient transportation to patients, visitors, students and employees within the UC Davis Medical Center campus. Routes connect the hospital with various buildings, parking lots, and other locations within the Medical Center area, including public transportation.

Routes & Schedules

All shuttle routes stop at the UC Davis Medical Center's Transit Center, located on "X" St. If your destination is serviced by another route, you may transfer to the appropriate shuttle from this terminal. Click on the tabs below for route information.
